Who We Are

LineWork Development specializes in high-end residential design-build projects throughout Los Angeles and Orange Counties. Founded by Eric Lin, we deliver seamless renovations, additions, ADUs, historic restorations, and ground-up construction with single-point accountability from concept through occupancy.

Our design-build approach eliminates coordination headaches while delivering superior craftsmanship, transparent budgeting, and accelerated timelines. We excel in complex projects requiring historic sensitivity, multigenerational living, luxury chef's kitchens, and sustainable systems integration.
